Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR) helps release physical tension.

Follow these steps to practice PMR:

  1. Start with your feet. Tense the muscles for 5 seconds, then relax.
  2. Move to your calves, thighs, stomach, arms, and neck, repeating the process.
  3. Focus on the feeling of relaxation in each muscle group.

Benefits of PMR:

  • Reduces Physical Tension
  • Calms the Mind
  • Promotes Emotional Resilience
  • Improves Sleep Quality
