Breathing Exercises

Breathing exercises are an effective way to reduce stress and improve relaxation !

Here are some common breathing techniques:

  1. Find a quiet place and sit comfortably.
  2. Take a deep breath in through your nose for 4 counts.
  3. Hold your breath for 7 counts.
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 8 counts.

How Breathing Exercises Help with Stress:

  • Activates the Parasympathetic Nervous System (PNS):Helps slow your heart rate, lower blood pressure, and promote relaxation.
  • Regulates Oxygen and Carbon Dioxide Levels:Controlled breathing restores this balance, improving mental clarity and physical calm.
  • Releases Muscle Tension: Proper breathing encourages physical relaxation, reducing muscle tension often caused by stress.
